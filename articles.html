Detailed Constitution Guide

Part I: The Union & its Territory (Art 1-4)

Art 1: Name and territory of the Union.
Art 2: Admission or establishment of new States.
Art 3: Formation of new States and alteration of areas, boundaries or names of existing States.
Art 4: Laws made under articles 2 and 3.

Part II: Citizenship (Art 5-11)

Art 5: Citizenship at the commencement of the Constitution.
Art 6-10: Rights of citizenship for migrants (Pakistan to India etc).
Art 11: Parliament to regulate the right of citizenship by law.

Part III: Fundamental Rights (Art 12-35)

Art 14: Equality before law.
Art 17: Abolition of Untouchability.
Art 19: Protection of 6 rights regarding freedom (Speech, assembly etc).
Art 21: Protection of life and personal liberty.
Art 21A: Right to education.
Art 23-24: Right against Exploitation.
Art 32: Remedies for enforcement of rights (Writ jurisdiction).

Part IV: Directive Principles (DPSP) (Art 36-51)

Art 39A: Equal justice and free legal aid.
Art 40: Organisation of village panchayats.
Art 44: Uniform civil code for the citizens.
Art 45: Provision for early childhood care and education.
Art 51: Promotion of international peace and security.

Part IV-A: Fundamental Duties (Art 51A)

Art 51A: List of 11 Fundamental Duties of every citizen of India.

Part V: The Union (Art 52-151)

Art 52: The President of India.
Art 63: The Vice-President of India.
Art 74: Council of Ministers to aid and advise President.
Art 76: Attorney-General for India.
Art 110: Definition of "Money Bills".
Art 124: Establishment and constitution of Supreme Court.
Art 148: Comptroller and Auditor-General (CAG) of India.

Part VI: The States (Art 152-237)

Art 153: Governors of States.
Art 165: Advocate-General for the State.
Art 213: Power of Governor to promulgate Ordinances.
Art 214: High Courts for States.
Art 226: Power of High Courts to issue certain writs.

Part XII: Finance, Property & Contracts (Art 264-300A)

Art 266: Consolidated Funds and Public Accounts of India and of the States.
Art 280: Finance Commission.
Art 300A: Persons not to be deprived of property save by authority of law (Right to Property).

Part IX: The Panchayats (Art 243-243O)

Art 243B: Constitution of Panchayats.
Art 243D: Reservation of seats.
Art 243G: Powers, authority and responsibilities of Panchayats.

Part XIV: Services under Union & States (Art 308-323)

Art 312: All-India services.
Art 315: Public Service Commissions (UPSC/SPSC) for the Union and for the States.
Art 320: Functions of Public Service Commissions.

Part XV: Elections (Art 324-329A)

Art 324: Superintendence, direction and control of elections to be vested in an Election Commission.
Art 326: Elections to the House of the People and to the Legislative Assemblies of States to be on the basis of adult suffrage.

Part XVII: Official Language (Art 343-351)

Art 343: Official language of the Union.
Art 348: Language to be used in the Supreme Court and in the High Courts.
Art 351: Directive for development of the Hindi language.

Part XVIII: Emergency Provisions (Art 352-360)

Art 352: Proclamation of Emergency (National Emergency).
Art 356: Provisions in case of failure of constitutional machinery in States (President's Rule).
Art 360: Provisions as to financial emergency.

Part XXI: Special Provisions (Art 369-392)

Art 370: Temporary provisions with respect to the State of Jammu and Kashmir (History).
Art 371: Special provision with respect to the States of Maharashtra and Gujarat.
Art 371A: Special provision with respect to the State of Nagaland.
